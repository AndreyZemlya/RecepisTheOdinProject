Buckwheat recepis

Simple step-by-step recipe for how to cook buckwheat having only buckwheat, a pot and seasonings.

What we need.

  • Buckwheat
  • A pot
  • Some seasonings

Cooking process

  1. Pour all the buckwheat into the pot
  2. Rinse the buckwheat 2-3 times in water.
  3. Fill the buckwheat with water so that there is 1.5 times more water than buckwheat.
  4. Put the pot on maximum heat and add the seasonings you want.
  5. Stir every 3 minutes and cook until boiling.
  6. Once boiling, simmer for another 5 minutes and turn off the heat
  7. Cover the pot with a lid and let the buckwheat stand for about 10-20 minutes.
  8. It's done! You can put it on plates and add some sauce and enjoy the buckwheat.
